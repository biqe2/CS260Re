Badi Quinteros Photography

I was born in Otavalo, Ecuador. I have been taking pictures since 2001. Taking photos is capturing moments for eternity. Those moments will be legacy for future generations. "Hapichi" means "to capture" in quichua and in pictures time is captured, and it bring us back to those unique experiences.
